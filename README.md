# Electric Two-Wheeler Design, Assembly, Testing & Validation Project

## Project Overview

This project documents the complete assembly, integration, testing, diagnostics, and validation of an Electric Two-Wheeler (EV) from the chassis stage to a fully operational vehicle. The project was carried out as part of Electric Vehicle Service Technician training and provided hands-on experience in EV architecture, electrical systems, safety procedures, troubleshooting, and technical documentation.

---

## Objectives

* Understand Electric Vehicle architecture and components.
* Assemble an Electric Two-Wheeler from chassis to final product.
* Perform electrical wiring and component integration.
* Conduct testing, diagnostics, and troubleshooting.
* Apply electrical safety practices.
* Develop technical documentation skills.

## Assembly Workflow

### 1. Chassis Preparation

Preparation and inspection of the vehicle frame before component installation.

### 2. Front Suspension and Wheel Assembly

Installation of front suspension components and wheel fitment.

### 3. Rear Suspension Installation

Mounting and alignment of rear suspension components.

### 4. Motor Installation

Installation of the BLDC Hub Motor for vehicle propulsion.

### 5. Battery Pack Installation

Mounting and securing the battery pack within the chassis.

### 6. Controller Placement

Installation of the motor controller and supporting electrical components.

### 7. Wiring Harness Integration

Connecting battery, controller, motor, throttle, lighting, and auxiliary systems.

### 8. Throttle and Handlebar Controls

Installation of rider control systems and dashboard components.

### 9. Brake System Installation

Assembly and inspection of braking components.

### 10. Lighting and Indicator Installation

Integration of headlamp, indicators, and electrical accessories.

### 11. Testing and Diagnostics

Performing voltage checks, continuity testing, and system diagnostics.

### 12. Final Assembly Inspection

Verification of electrical and mechanical systems before operation.

### 13. Vehicle Validation

Testing the completed vehicle for safe and reliable operation.
